Hand Gesture Recognition: Instructions

How to Use

  • Ensure your camera is connected and enabled.
  • The live video feed will display your hand gestures.
  • Detected gestures appear below the video feed.
  • Use the "Privacy Mode" toggle to disable/enable the camera at any time.
  • Review recent detections and provide feedback to help improve the model.

Giving Feedback

  • Click ✔️ Correct if the detected gesture is accurate.
  • Click ❌ Incorrect and enter the correct gesture if the detection is wrong.

Troubleshooting

  • No video feed? Make sure your camera is connected and not used by another app.
  • Camera Disabled? Check the "Privacy Mode" toggle and ensure it's off for detection.
  • Gestures not detected? Ensure your hand is visible and well-lit in the camera frame.
  • Still having issues? Try refreshing the page or restarting the server.
